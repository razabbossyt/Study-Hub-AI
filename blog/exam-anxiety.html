Main Advice

Reduce anxiety by reducing uncertainty

Exam anxiety usually gets worse when preparation feels unclear. Confidence comes from evidence, so your study plan should show exactly what has been covered and what still needs attention.

Use simple routines before the exam: shorter review, predictable sleep, and breathing or grounding exercises that you have already practiced before the day itself.

  • Reduce uncertainty with a written revision checklist.
  • Practice under timed conditions to make the format familiar.
  • Do not judge progress by stress level alone.
