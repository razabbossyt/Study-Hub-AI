Main Takeaway

What AI tools should actually do

AI tools help most when they remove repetitive work. Summarizing text, turning notes into flashcards, or building a study plan saves time that can be spent on actual understanding.

The quality bar still matters. Students should treat AI output as a first draft, then edit it to match the syllabus, teacher style, and exam format.

  • Use AI to structure information first.
  • Review facts before relying on generated answers.
  • Choose the tool that matches the task instead of forcing one tool to do everything.
