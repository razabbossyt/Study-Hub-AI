Flashcard Strategy

How to use flashcards the right way

Flashcards work best when each card tests one idea. Keep the question specific and the answer short enough to recall without guessing what the card wants.

Review easy cards less often and difficult cards more often. The power of flashcards comes from spacing and retrieval, not from creating a huge deck in one day.

  • Write prompts that force a clear answer.
  • Avoid copying long textbook sentences onto cards.
  • Mark weak cards and review them sooner.
