Speed Strategy

How to revise a chapter faster without losing focus

Fast revision comes from reducing friction. Decide what you need to know, break the chapter into small chunks, and switch from reading to testing as early as possible.

Use a short cycle: skim, summarize, test, then move on. If you keep rewriting the same content, revision feels busy but not effective.

  • Set a target outcome for each session before you start.
  • Convert paragraphs into brief notes or bullets immediately.
  • Use a timer to stop low-value over-review.
