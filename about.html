What is Study Hub AI

A Free Platform Built
for Every Student

Study Hub AI is a free collection of AI-powered study tools designed specifically for school and university students. Whether you need structured notes, revision flashcards, a study schedule, or help answering exam questions — every tool is right here.

We believe learning should be accessible to everyone. That's why every single tool on Study Hub AI is completely free — no account required, no payment walls, no limits. Just open a tool and start studying.

From generating clear notes from messy paragraphs to building an entire revision schedule, Study Hub AI puts the power of artificial intelligence directly in the hands of students who need it most.

Our Origin Story
"We built Study Hub AI because every student deserves the same powerful tools — regardless of budget."
Study Hub AI started with a simple observation: students who had access to good revision tools consistently performed better — but most of those tools were expensive, complicated, or scattered across dozens of different apps.

We set out to change that. Our mission from day one has been to build free, student-focused AI tools that are genuinely useful — not bloated with unnecessary features or hidden behind a signup wall.

Every decision we make — from the tools we build to the way we design them — is guided by one question: does this actually help a student learn better? If the answer is yes, we build it.
