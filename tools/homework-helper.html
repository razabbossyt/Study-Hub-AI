Tips for Better Homework Help

Get the most accurate explanations with these techniques.

Be specific with your question
Instead of "explain history", ask "What were the causes of the French Revolution?" Specific questions get much better answers.
Use the Key Points as a checklist
Each key point is evidence you can expand in your real exam. Add your own examples and supporting details to each one.
Read, don't just copy
Use the answer as a learning scaffold. Read it, understand it, then write your own version — that's how you retain it.
Match detail level to question type
"State" questions → Concise. "Explain", "Discuss", "Evaluate" → Detailed. Longer questions earn more marks and need more depth.
