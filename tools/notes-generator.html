How It Works

From raw text to structured study notes in seconds — no formatting, no effort.

Tips for Better Notes

Get the most out of the Notes Generator with these simple techniques.

One topic at a time
Paste text about a single topic for the clearest notes. Mixing topics can reduce the quality of the output.
More text = better notes
The tool works best with 100+ words. Very short inputs may not have enough content to produce useful notes.
Try different detail levels
Use Brief for a quick overview, Detailed for deep study sessions, or Medium for everyday revision.
Edit after generating
Use the generated notes as your starting point. Copy to your notes app and add your own highlights and context.
