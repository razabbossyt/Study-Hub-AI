Tips for the Best Bullet Points

Get cleaner, more useful results with these quick techniques.

Use full, complete paragraphs
The converter works sentence by sentence — pasting complete paragraphs with proper punctuation gives you the cleanest and most accurate bullet points.
Match split mode to your content
Use Sentence for essays, Line break for lists or bullet notes, and Paragraph for long multi-section texts with distinct topics.
Keep duplicate removal on
Academic texts often repeat the same idea in slightly different ways. The duplicate removal toggle filters near-identical sentences so your bullets stay concise.
Use numbered style for sequences
If your text describes steps, a process, or a timeline, switch to the Numbered style — it makes the order clearer and easier to follow when revising.
