Tips for Better Essays

Get the most out of the rewriter with these techniques.

One paragraph at a time
Rewriting focused sections gives the AI more context and produces sharper, more accurate results than pasting an entire essay.
Use "Simplify" for academic jargon
Heavy academic language often obscures meaning. Simplify mode removes filler words and replaces jargon with plain English.
Always review the diff view
The Changes tab shows exactly what the AI altered. Review every change to make sure your original meaning is preserved.
Use "Formal" for academic submissions
The Formal mode adjusts tone and vocabulary to match academic or professional writing standards expected by markers.
