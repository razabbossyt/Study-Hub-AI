Quick Study Tips
1
Study in 25-min blocks then take a 5-min break. Your brain consolidates better in short sprints.
2
Write notes by hand first then type them up. The double-pass forces you to process information deeper.
3
Test yourself constantly. Closing the book and recalling is 3× more effective than re-reading.
4
Space your revision — review after 1 day, 3 days, then 7 days for maximum long-term retention.
